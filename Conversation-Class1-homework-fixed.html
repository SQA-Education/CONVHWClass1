1. Conversation Awareness

Starting Conversations in American Social Settings

In many American social settings, conversations often begin with small talk about work, hobbies, neighborhoods, or weekend plans.

Friendly behavior does not always mean a close friendship right away. People often take time to build trust.

Good conversationalists usually ask follow-up questions, share a little about themselves, and avoid heavy topics too early.
